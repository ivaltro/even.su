What does the EVEN Group of Companies do?

EVEN LTD is a group that includes several enterprises in the automotive and sports industries.

Founded in 2006 by a group of entrepreneurs working in the field of freight transportation, EVEN primarily became known for its company EVEN Truck, which supplied high-quality hydraulic equipment under the A.M.P hydraulic brand.

In 2023, the EVEN Group expanded its product line to include semi-trailers, special vehicles and superstructures under the TITAN® brand, as well as the supply of commercial vehicles, including trucks and light trucks.

«EVEN Truck» – hydrofication of tractors

«EVEN Truck» is a company specializing in the production and servicing of hydraulic equipment for dump trucks. The company manufactures its products in its own factories using modern technologies and adheres to all necessary quality standards.

Since 2023, the production of its own range of loading equipment has been launched, including multilifts, crane manipulators, and all types of trailers. In addition, the company is actively involved in the supply of various tractor units from China.

«SHAFT» – transmission for commercial vehicles

«SHAFT» is one of the largest players in the market for the sale of spare parts for trucks and buses. Since 2008, the company has been engaged in the sale of reliable, high-tech spare parts for most domestic and foreign commercial vehicles.

The company has three wholesale warehouses in Russia: in Moscow, Yekaterinburg, with a total area of 3 100 m3.

«TITAN» – annual triathlon competitions

The «TITAN» races are organized by the «EVEN» Group of Companies and the Federal Sports Training System «Cyclone» with the aim of popularizing sports and engaging a large number of country residents in a unique hobby – triathlon.

Our amateur competitions are held only on perfect tracks in picturesque locations. Our favorite venue is located near Lake Belskoye in the center of the city of Bronnitsy.

«TITAN TRUCK» — полуприцепы для эффективного бизнеса

«TITAN TRUCK» is a Russian brand of semi-trailers from the EVEN company.

We produce a full line of semi-trailers: flatbed, curtain-sided, refrigerators, container carriers and others. We offer high quality, any modifications at the request of the customer, fast deliveries.

We also offer superstructures for special equipment and commercial vehicles (tractors, light-duty vehicles).
